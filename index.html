The Yamaha TZR250 is a motorcycle manufactured and produced by the Japanese motorcycle manufacturer Yamaha between 1986 and 1995.

  • Power: 45 hp (34 kW) (restricted)
  • Wheelbase: 54.1 in (1,370 mm)
  • Weight: 280 lb (127 kg)[1] (dry) / 320 lb (150 kg)[1] (wet)

Yamaha produced the road going two-stroke motorcycle, loosely based on the TZ250 Yamaha racing bike (resource from wikipedia).
